Account Creation

When opening the app for the first time, you will be prompted to create an account. After entering a valid email address and strong password, press register.

Set Idle Limit

The first thing you want to do is set an idling limit. Press the Settings icon, select Goals, and scroll to set a personal goal. Select litres or gallons. This is simply used as a benchmark for your statistics.

Add Vehicle

To add a vehicle, press the circular add icon on the bottom navigation bar.

Pick an icon and enter a nickname, brand, and fuel consumption details. The nickname and brand details are for your personal organization, and you can lookup your vehicle fuel consumption online by searching “fuel economy” + your vehicle model.

Press add and you should now be able to see the vehicle on your main drive page. You can add several vehicles.

Starting Trip

To start a trip, press your vehicle card. A timer will show up, and press the "play" button to start the timer. Trips will start recording immediately after you press the button.

Trip Review

After your trip is finished, press the stop button and you will be prompted to review the places you stopped. If you idled in that location, meaning that your engine was running but you were not moving, please press the “yes” button. After you have reviewed all points, your trip will be added.

Stats

You can view your trip details in the stats page. It is organized into daily, monthly, and all time information.

Edit Vehicles

To edit vehicle information, press the icon in the top right of the vehicle card and select edit. The current vehicle information is displayed.
