SwitchOff App Privacy Policy And User Protection

The SwitchOff Team built the SwitchOff mobile application as a free-of-charge application. This SERVICE is for use as-is. This privacy policy is used to inform potential users regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information for all users of the SERVICE.

Information Collected

The application collects user emails for the purposes of account registration so that users can access individual data.

The idle tracker uses location services, for the purpose of displaying places idled to the user. This information is available to the user only at the time of trip review, and is not accessible by other users or third party organizations.

The application uses Google Analytics to collect statistics on how the app is used for the improvement and development of the application (e.g. the amount of times an in-app functionality is used). Usage information is used to ensure proper maintenance and engagement of the mobile application.

Any idle location information displayed is unattached to the user account. Your account information will not be associated with any location points, for the protection and privacy of all users.

Information Privacy

SwitchOff will not and will not assist or permit any third party app to pass information to Google that can be recognized or personally identifiable information.

User Protection

Your consent is required for the use of this application, as it is necessary for the operation of the SERVICE to use location and account permissions. By agreeing to the Privacy Policy, you are consenting to the SERVICE in handling your location and account data, compliant with the Privacy Policy. By disagreeing to the Privacy Policy, you are unable to make use of the SERVICE as all permissions are essential for the application usage. If you have pre-existing data with the SERVICE and no longer consent to the Privacy Policy, you may contact support@switchoffapp.com to erase your data. You have the right, at any time, to view your account data, accessed by contacting support@switchoffapp.com.

Ads

The SwitchOff mobile application does not contain advertisements and is not connected to Google Admob.

Policy Application And Validity

This Policy is applicable to all usage of the SERVICE and data stored therein. Changes made to the privacy policy will be updated and users will be notified in-app. The updated Privacy Policy will always be available on the application website.
