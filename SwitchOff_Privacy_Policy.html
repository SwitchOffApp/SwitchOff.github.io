SwitchOff App Privacy Policy And User Protection

Effective Date: 25 March 2020
Policy Version: 1.0

support@switchoffapp.com | www.switchoffapp.com

The SwitchOff Application consists of the SwitchOff Mobile Application and the SwitchOff website (each as defined hereinafter and collectively the “Application”). SwitchOff (“we”, “us”) acknowledges and understands the importance of user (“you”) privacy and is committed to protecting and securely storing all personal information.

Please read our Privacy Policy carefully as it explains:

  • Collection of personal information
  • Use of personal information
  • Protection of personal information
  • Rights to access, rectify, and erase personal information

SwitchOff asks all users to assume responsibility for safeguarding any authentication information. Do not allow any third parties to access your personal account on the SwitchOff Application. If you suspect any unauthorized use, immediately notify support@switchoffapp.com.

By accepting this Privacy Policy, you acknowledge and agree to our Privacy Policy. By disagreeing to the Privacy Policy, you are unable to use the SwitchOff App in any manner as all permissions are essential for the application usage.

Information Collection and Use

  • The Application collects user emails and passwords for the purposes of account registration so that users can access individual data.
  • The Application may also collect information about your device, such as its model, unique device identifier and operating system version, through a crash report for the purposes of diagnosing and managing technical issues.
  • SwitchOff App is an idle tracker that uses location services, for the purpose of displaying idle locations to the user. This information is available to the user only at the time of trip review, and is not accessible by other users or third party organizations.
  • The Application uses Google Analytics to collect statistics on how the app and website are used for maintenance, improvement, and development. Agree to the Analytics Terms of Service (please review them as they may have changed since you last viewed them).

We guarantee that any idle location information displayed is unattached to the user account. Your account information will not be associated with any location points, for the protection and privacy of all users.

Information Privacy and Protection

SwitchOff will not permit the disclosure of personally identifiable information to any unauthorized Third Party. We value your trust and will take appropriate measures on the administrative and technical level to protect your Personal Information.

In the unlikely event of a privacy breach, SwitchOff is required to notify the Office of the Information and Privacy Commissioner (OIPC), which may require user notification if the breach is considered to pose a significant risk to individual privacy.

User Rights

  • If you have pre-existing data and no longer consent to the Privacy Policy, you may contact support@switchoffapp.com to erase your data.
  • You have the right to request correction of an identified error or omission in personal information by contacting support@switchoffapp.com.
  • You have the right, at any time, to view your account data, accessed by contacting support@switchoffapp.com.

Updates or Changes to Privacy Policy

Changes made to the Privacy Policy will be updated and users will be notified in-app. The updated Privacy Policy will always be available on the application website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@switchoffapp.com.
