About us


We want to:
1. Give users a way to see how much they idle;
2. Visualize and personalize the impact of idling;
3. Educate and motivate users to set goals and reduce their idling.

The Team

All of our team members are students. We started as an all-female team high school project for an entry to the Technovation Challenge, then proceeded to pursue it to launch after placing as worldwide semifinalists.
