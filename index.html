The App

SwitchOff is a simple mobile application that seeks the impact of idling and reduce vehicle idling by providing users real and personal data from their car's idle time.

Features

Location Services

to find out where people idle most.

Trip Calculator

to estimate how your idling contributes to fuel wastage, money lost, and emissions.

Live Trip Timer

to give real data and help you be aware of your idle time.

Learn about switchoff



Here at SwitchOff, our aim is to reduce idle-related vehicle emissions by making use of the technology that people have access to. We want to build awareness and proactiveness through the use of our mobile application.
